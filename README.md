# MokouScript
Stand API Lua script. If I would get any other menus, though unlikely, I may port it for them if needed. 

## Current Functions
Cinderella: A rendition of Cinderella from NIKKE : Goddess of Victory. The Nikke who instilled fear in mankind as the Heretic Anachiro. She showers compliments upon everything that she deems beautiful.

Rapunzel: A saint-like figure who appeases the spirits of those who have died on the surface. Often found lost in prayer, she is also prone to reminiscence.

Vehicle Buff: Buff vehicles to stronger health. May or may not work at this moment.
